Our Story

Vorskins was founded in August 1991 and located in Brighton, England. It was founded by Erika Felia Oroh who believe that business can be used as a power to help each other. The business is inspired by her concerns about the make up at that time that use so many chemical that prove to have a significant risk to skin in a long term. And with that she start question herself, why can’t we have a better and more save make up? , then she came to a conclusion that, if other people can’t offer her what she need, why don’t make your own?. And that exact time, as if there is a lighting that struck her head that she began to see the opportunity.


Our Vision

As what our founder believed, we believe that business have a power to help those in need and can bring changes to make the world a better place.


Our Mission

We want to provide a safe and affordable make up and skin care to the world so that every women can be beautiful in her own way without having to risk her skin health.
